Diploma in Agriculture

Agriculture remains the main segment of the economy that contributes the largest share into the nation's GDP (20%). Diploma in Agriculture is a full time course of 2-year duration which offers study about various topics regarding agriculture.
This program deals with basic methodologies of farming. There are various specializations which are offered in the Diploma in Agriculture course such as seed technology, horticulture, animal husbandry, organic farming, etc.

Diploma in Agriculture: What is it about?

The following are some of the key facts for Diploma in Agriculture

  • It is a diploma level course offered to the candidates interested in agriculture and its relevant research field.
  • The duration of the course is 2 years and the curriculum is divided into 4 semesters.

Diploma in Agriculture Eligibility Criteria

Students who wish to pursue a Diploma in Agriculture course must fulfil the following eligibility criteria.

  • The candidate must have passed Class 10
  • The candidate must have a minimum of 50% marks in aggregate.

Diploma in Agriculture: Specialization

Diploma in Agriculture offers specializations in various fields such as:

  • Food Processing – In this specialization, students are made to learn about several Food processing procedures, different machinery units involved in mega production, different techniques, etc.
  • Seed Technology – The candidates are made to learn several techniques regarding seed, the measures for quality, organic farming procedures, advantages of organic farming, improvements in seed planting techniques, etc.
  • Animal Husbandry – This course offers study about various aspects of agriculture and covers several sub concepts regarding agriculture, harvesting, farming, etc. so that farming and agriculture techniques can be improved.
  • Organic Farming – This concept has become very famous nowadays as it helps one to do farming without using fertilizers and pesticides. The candidates are made to learn about chemical free farming through this specialization.

Diploma in Agriculture: Jobs and Salary

  • A wide range of careers await those who have completed Diploma in Agriculture course in both the private and public sectors.
  • There are a large number of job opportunities for students in this field. Agriculture Diploma holders are hired in many different sectors of the industry such as Food production firms, Government agricultural firms, Banks, Plantations, Government fertilizer manufacturing firms etc.
  • Fresh Diploma holders of this course can easily expect an initial salary of INR 2,00,000 to 10,00,000 per year. The salary offered is proportional to the experience earned in the field. The salary offered increases with increase in the experience in the field.

Diploma in Agriculture: Future Scope

Diploma in Agriculture program is gaining popularity nowadays and offers diverse career opportunities. This further affirms the immense popularity of the course among students. The most popular choices of education on completion of a Diploma in Agriculture are as follows.

  • BScIf one wishes to continue in the same field of education, the first program of choice is a BSc Agriculture. It is a three-year course and the eligibility criteria include passing Class 12 in relevant streams.
  • PGDA large number of Diploma holders choose to go for the PGDM course. Admissions are offered through a national level entrance test.
  • Competitive examinations: Another route that Diploma holders can go for is to prepare for competitive examinations. Examinations that are meant for job opportunities in Government sector organizations are most popular. Jobs in this sector are secure with assured high pay and regular increments.
